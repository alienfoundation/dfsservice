About

What We Believe?

Here at DFS, we believe in transforming the complex technologies into simple and affordable solutions which can be used by everyone to create a safer world.

Who We Are?

Digital Fire and Security Services (DFS Services) is an Indian startup working with MCDI Canada and DFS UK for Technology sharing, R&D, Product design, and development.

How We Started?

Beside better living standards and improved technology, our society is less safe. We live in constant fear and worry about the safety of our home, office, or loved ones. Well, we decided to solve this issue and thats how we started.

What We Offer

Security Grid

The most reliable and trusted solution to keep your home and business safe from the traumatic incidents such as robbery, fire accident, theft, and medical emergency.

Video Door Phone

You can never know the intentions of the stranger standing outside your home. And once the door is open, you can be his next victim! A video door phone allows you to view and talk to that stranger without actually opening the door.
